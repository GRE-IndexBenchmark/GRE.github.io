About GRE

GRE is a benchmark suite for learned indexes and traditional indexes to measure throughput and latency with custom workload (read / write ratio) and any dataset. GRE quantifies datasets using local and global hardness, and includes a synthetic data generator to generate data with various hardness.

The GRE benchmark is brought to you by Eric Lo and Chaichon Wongkham, from CUHK

This method is aimed at providing a comprehensive and fair benchmarking toward different Learned Index in different database environment

The index developers can detect their products' performance and reliability in varied database difficulty
