Note: Throughput heatmap can show the throughput ratio between the best performing learned index and the best performing tradtional index.

The three axes in the map represent the local/global hardness of a dataset and the write ratio respectively.

The balls in the heatmap represent the best performed index inside certain datasets at particular wirte ratio. A red/yellow ratio means a traditional index is the winner, a blue one means a learned index is the winner. The darker a ball is, the more advantage this index takes.
